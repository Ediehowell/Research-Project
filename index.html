The Evolution of MacBooks

  • 1991: Powerbook 100 Released

    Source:Luke Dormehl, PowerBook 100 fueled a laptop revolution, cultofmac.com

  • Released: October 21, 1991
  • Set-back keyboard; integrated palm rest; trackball
  • Price: $2,500

  • 1994: Powerbook 500 Released

    Source:PowerBook 500 series, Wikipedia.org

  • Released: May 1994
  • Trackpad; built-in microphone; built-in Ethernet; sterio speakers
  • Price:$2,270

  • 1999: iBook G3 Released

    Source:Apple iBook G3/366 (Grey), computinghistory.org

  • Released: June 21, 1999
  • Wireless networking; PowerPC G3 Processor; 6-hour lithium ion battery
  • $1,599

  • 2001:Powerbook Titanium G4 Released

    Source:Apple PowerBook G4 500 (Original - Ti) Specs, everymac.com

  • Released: January 2001
  • PowerPC G4 Processor; titanium metal body
  • $2,599

  • 2008: MacBook Air Released

    Source:Adrien Ramirez, Apple Macbook Air, usatoday.com

  • Released: January 2008
  • World's thinnest notebook
  • $1,799
